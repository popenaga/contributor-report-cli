# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Participants in this project are expected to communicate respectfully and constructively.

Examples of expected behavior:

- Being respectful in discussions and reviews
- Accepting feedback in good faith
- Focusing criticism on code and behavior, not people
- Avoiding harassment, discrimination, and personal attacks

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment or abusive language
- Discriminatory comments
- Deliberate intimidation
- Publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation for behavior that harms the project or its community.
